Banana ice cream

  • 2 bananas
  • a few spoons of yoghurt
  • chocolate chips
  1. slice the bananas
  2. place the sliced bananas in a freezer bag
  3. put the bag in the freezer
  4. after 3 hours, take them out of the freezer
  5. put the frozen banana sliced in a blender
  6. add the yoghurt
  7. add the chocolate chips
  8. blitz it!
